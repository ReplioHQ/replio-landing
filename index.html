The HEARD Framework

Every response built on the framework that turns guests around.

The HEARD model is the hospitality standard inside Replio. Every AI-drafted response follows it — across every platform, every review, every time.

A complaint resolved the right way makes a guest 70% more likely to return than someone who never had a problem. That's not just hospitality. That's revenue.

H

Hear

Read the full review before typing anything. Actually understand what the guest experienced.

E

Empathize

Lead with how they felt. "We're sorry you feel that way" is a hedge. That's not empathy — and guests know the difference.

A

Apologize

A real one. No conditions. No deflection. Own it — even when it's hard.

R

Resolve

Tell them what's changing, or personally invite them back. This is the Second Mile moment.

D

Delight

Don't just get back to neutral. Leave them better than you found them. That's the standard.
